Why Tutoring Is Important

  • Fixing Individual gaps

    If there are gaps in the teaching material, if students are struggling with subjects or certain contents, or if they are preparing for specific exams, tutoring can go beyond the possibilities of school teaching.

  • Building Self Confidence For Better Performance

    Another reason why tutoring is important is because it is able to prevent or break a vicious circle. Regardless of the cause of a student having accumulated gaps in the learning material or worrying about the grades, tutoring can help the student get on his own again.

  • Tutoring teaches transfer skills

    Whether they take tutoring with a teacher or deal with the school material online at home makes little difference. It is important that they deal with the material in the first place beyond the 45 minutes of lessons and get the time they need to really understand the material, rather than being able to recite it on demand.

  • Tutoring Intensifies The School Learning Process

    Online tutoring is conceivable, which is more fun for school children than sitting with a tutor. In this way, they apply what they have learned in practice and gain experience that gives them more security at the next exam when they are sitting in front of new use cases.
